Abstract

This ontology describes an alignment of the W3C Semantic Sensor Network (SSN( Incubator Group's SSN Ontology and the W3C Provenance Working Group's PROV-O ontoloyg. The alignment extends the expressive capability of the SSNO for recording observations and system deployments, enabling more comprehensive historical information to be described than is possible using SSNO alone.

For further details about the design of the ontology, please see

Compton, M. and Corsar, D. and Taylor, K. Sensor Data Provenance:SSNO and PROV-O Together At Last. Joint Proceedings of the 6th International Workshop on the Foundations, Technologies and Applications of the Geospatial Web and 7th International Workshop on Semantic Sensor Networks. Vol 1404 CEUR Workshop Proceedings. 2014. http://ceur-ws.org/Vol-1401/paper-05.pdf
